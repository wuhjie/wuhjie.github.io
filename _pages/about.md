I’m Hongjie Wu, a Graduate Research Assistant at COLALab, University of Exeter. My research interests lie in agentic reasoning, interpretable AI, and structured decision-making systems. I am interested in building AI systems that can infer hidden task structure, reason over state and action dynamics, and use feedback or verification to improve their decisions in complex environments. I recently completed my master’s studies at Imperial College London and Queen Mary University of London, and previously did my bachelor’s degree at the University of Bristol.
